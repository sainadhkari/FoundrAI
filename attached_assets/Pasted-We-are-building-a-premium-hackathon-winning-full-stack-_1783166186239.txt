We are building a premium hackathon-winning full-stack AI application called FoundrAI.

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
FoundrAI is an AI Executive War Room for founders and startup teams.

Users submit startup ideas, and 10 AI agents analyze the business idea from multiple perspectives:

* Market Opportunity
* Competitor Analysis
* Financial Risk
* Customer Psychology
* Product Strategy
* Growth Potential
* Investment Potential
* Execution Complexity
* Business Risks

Final verdict:

* Proceed
* Pivot
* Reject
